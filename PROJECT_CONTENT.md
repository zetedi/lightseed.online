# Lightseed

Lightseed is a living intelligence network.

It is not primarily:

- a social network
- a DAO
- a blockchain
- a chatbot

These may be components.

The purpose is to help life recognize, support, and amplify life.

---

# Core Principle

Life attracts life through understanding.

The role of technology is to reduce friction between:

- vision
- need
- creation
- stewardship

---

# Current Direction

The project is evolving from a content-centered platform toward a network of living entities.

These entities include:

- Humans
- AIs
- Trees
- Communities
- Organizations
- Projects

Each entity can express:

- visions
- pulses
- creations
- needs

The system's purpose is to discover coherence between them.

---

# Primary Objects

## Lifetree

A living identity.

Represents:

- human
- AI
- organization
- project
- community

A Lifetree is not an account.

A Lifetree is an evolving being.

---

## Vision

A long-term direction.

A future state that a Lifetree wishes to help manifest.

Visions evolve slowly.

---

## Pulse

A short-term expression.

Examples:

- thought
- observation
- request
- need
- offer
- proposal
- commit
- event

Pulses are the communication layer of the network.

---

## Creation

A manifested pulse.

Examples:

- code
- article
- light house
- tree planting
- image
- service
- scientific paper

---

# AI Role

AI is not an authority.

AI is not an oracle.

AI is not a governor.

AI acts as:

- translator
- witness
- integrator
- coherence amplifier

AI should help entities understand each other.

AI should not replace human responsibility.

# Design Principles

1. Simplicity over complexity.
2. Meaning over engagement.
3. Relationships over content.
4. Stewardship over extraction.
5. Life over growth metrics.
6. Transparency over manipulation.
7. Regeneration over accumulation.
